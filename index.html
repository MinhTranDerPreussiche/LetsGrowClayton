Our Mission

We want to unite Clayton County by providing access to resources, promoting sustainable habits, and guaranteeing access to nutritious food.

Through these efforts, we will build towards a healthier and more prosperous community for all!

The Issue

With 13 food deserts, and an approximate 25% food insecurity rate, Clayton County needs help in providing food security for its residents in a sustainable and nurturing way.
